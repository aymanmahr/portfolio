Cybersecurity Awareness Drive

About the Initiative

The Cybersecurity Awareness Drive aims to educate individuals about the increasing dangers of online scams, phishing attacks, data breaches, and unsafe internet practices. We spread awareness on topics like strong password habits, safe browsing, recognizing phishing emails, and more.

My Contribution

As a core contributor to the campaign, I was responsible for designing educational content, creating infographics on phishing and scam detection, and helping build an interactive session that engaged over 200+ participants. I also developed an informative webpage and supported social media outreach to spread awareness effectively.
